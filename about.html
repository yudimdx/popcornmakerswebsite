About

Popcorn Makers

This company was founded in 7th February, 2021 and its aim is to connect with millions of customers by allowing them to make delicious popcorn in minutes by selling them popcorn makers machine. It is achieved by the operation with a button / a button makes it extremely easy to cook popcorns. The procedure is to put some kernels in the popcorn chamber, turn it on and wait 3 minutes and get 15 cups of delicious fragrant oil-less popcorn.


There is an indicator button as well which turns red once the cooking time has been reached. The timer will beep a couple of times to let you know when it's done. Moreover, the surface of the popping chamber is BPA free and its material is made up of food grade aluminium alloy. This means its durable conduct abs allows is to be resistant to bumps and scratch, while delivering popcorns.
